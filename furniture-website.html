About Our Workshop

15 Years of Craftsmanship

Founded in 2010, InteriorBsc Woodworks has been creating beautiful, functional furniture for homes and businesses across the region. Our master craftsmen combine traditional techniques with modern design to produce pieces that stand the test of time.

We source only the finest materials, with a focus on sustainable and locally-sourced wood. Each piece is carefully constructed to ensure it becomes a cherished part of your home for generations to come.
